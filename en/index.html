SIP-enabled IP video door station

A unique experience

Enjoy full colour video and high-quality digital audio in wall mounted Iddero indoor monitors, IP videophones, or directly on your smarphone or tablet.

Main features

  • 1 Colour camera with night mode LED illumination
  • 2 Full duplex audio with acoustic echo cancellation
  • 3 Available with 1, 2, or 4 direct call buttons
  • 4 Optional keypad: Up to 999 users + direct input of lock codes
MIP-TK4C
  • Up to 5 call destinations per user; parallel or sequential calling
  • 2 onboard relays + control of 2 external (IP) relays
  • Custom audio messages
  • Supports external SIP servers and direct (P2P) calls

Attractive. Durable. Versatile.

MURA IP features a neutral design with straight and clean lines. Its attractive and minimalist style is in perfect harmony with contemporary architecture. The body is made of anodized aluminium, a material that was selected for its aesthetic value and exceptional outdoor resistance: MURA IP is built to last.

Easy installation

MURA IP can be easily and quickly installed on any surface. No wall mounting box is required. A single Ethernet cable combines power and data. Easy configuration through a standard web browser.
